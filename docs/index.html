5eCollector
A Discord Bot which is based on 5e.tools monsters. Free and Open Source.
Tutorial
5eTools is a bot focused on collecting monsters from the 5e.tools Bestiary. To start the bot, run (needs admin permissions).
col init
Now anyone on the server can start catching monsters! Running
col me
gives you an overview over your user. This includes rolls and catches. Now we can start finding some monsters! Run
col roll
several times and react to the message of any monster you would like to catch.
Running
col me
not only reveals your new monsters but the fact that your rolls and catches reset after a certain duration.
Running
col view [monster-id]
gives you an overview of any creature (not just your own!). The "monster-id" can be found to the left of all of your creatures. For example in
#12 Mimic [Cr: 2]
The ID is 12.
Wondering what all those stats like STR and AC mean? Combat.
Any member of a server can choose a Chosen who will gather Glory which will converted back into points once that chosen is defeated or replaced. Run
col chosen [monster-id]
to select your chosen. They will now gather Glory, the longer they have been your chosen, the more glory they generate.
A Chosen's glory streak is broken once they brought to zero hp or replaced by a different chosen. That means it is in your interest to defeat your friends' Chosen (if you want to stop them from gaining points at least).
col attack [player] [your-monster-id] [stat:("str"|"dex"|"con"|"int"|"wis"|"cha")]
Combat goes through three phases:
Phase 1: Your creature makes a skill-check against the Chosen. If they win, they proceed to the next phase.
Phase 2: Your creature makes a skill-check against the AC (Armor Class) of the Chosen. If they win, they proceed to the next phase.
Phase 3: Your creature makes a skill-check to determine the damage they deal to the Chosen. They also have the chance to points from the Chosen's owner if the Chosen has a lot of glory.
Reference
col help
Gives you a link to this page.
col me
Gives you an overview of your user.
col user [user]
Gives you an overview of a user.
col view [monster-id|monster-type]
Gives you an overview of a monster (by ID) or a monster-type (case sensitive).
col rename [monster-id] [name]
Rename a monster.
col roll
Roll for a new creature
col stats ["points"|"monster"|"glory"]
Shows the ranking by a certain statistic.
col chosen [monster-id]
Choose a monster as your new chosen. Choosing the current chosen makes them retreat.
col attack [player] [your-monster-id] [stat:("str"|"dex"|"con"|"int"|"wis"|"cha")]
Attack a player's chosen.
col trade [your-monster-id] [other-monster-id]
Trade one of your monsters for another.
col combine [monster-id] [monster-id] [monster-id]
Combine three monsters of equal type and level into a monster of a higher level.
